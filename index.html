AI Artist

Our AI was trained on a collection of thousands of famous paintings to learn how to create new art like a real painter would. The paintings it makes do not already exist, they are computer generated art.


These paintings are genreated using a GAN (Generative Adversarial Network), where two neural networks compete with each other in a game. One NN creates fake art and the other NN acts as a critic and tries to figure out which images are real and which aren't. Through this process, the art creating NN gets better and better over time at producing fakes.


Our AI Paintings were created using the following program: https://github.com/pbaylies/stylegan2.
